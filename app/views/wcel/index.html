Work coach email link testing

What we want to learn

  • Email templates tested well. The ‘failed to attend’ template was unexpected, but only because they do not currently have a process for people who miss their appointment.
  • Users could articulate what the link to the templates would do, and understood what to do with the pre-populated email when it opened. One person was a little surprised that they still had to attach documents.
  • ‘General email’ was not clear - they suggested words like ‘enquiry’ and ‘query’
