Version 3 – BA visit on 16 May to Burnley

Changes in this version

  • Removed ‘fail to attend’ status
  • Content made clearer to show that action is required by the agent before the claim can be verified, with a checkbox added to ensure action is taken before moving on
  • Changed the definition of ‘verified’ – this is now based on proving identity, rather than supplying all evidence needed
  • Added the time next to the date that the application was submitted
  • Designed validation messages for testing

What we learned

  • Staff were concerned that the ‘special rules’ banner might get missed and suggested it should be made more prominent
  • Processors in Burnley won’t be uploading the PDF until the application has been processed (this is different to the process we thought would happen in Belfast)
