Have they proved their identity?

Help with identity checks

Fred should bring 3 identity documents:

  • 1 current primary document, such as a passport or driving licence
  • 2 original secondary documents, such as a recent bank statement, utility bill or council tax statement

If they have not got enough proof of identity, you will need to do biographical checks.

More guidance on accepted identity documents and how to do biographical checks is available in the DWP common standards for identity verification (opens in a new tab).
