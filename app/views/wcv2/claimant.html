Documents to check at the appointment

Identity documents

You will need to see:

  • 1 current primary identity document
  • 2 original secondary identity documents
List of primary and secondary documents

Primary identity documents:

  • UK passport
  • EEA passport
  • UK photo driving licence
  • national identity card
  • residence permit or card
  • immigration status document
  • registration or naturalisation certificate

Secondary identity documents:

  • birth certificate
  • recent bank or building society statement
  • council tax statement
  • NHS medical card
  • debit or charge card
  • expired passport
  • expired UK driving licence
  • local authority rent card
  • marriage or civil partnership certificate
  • registration or naturalisation certificate
  • tenancy agreement

If they do not have their identity documents, you can do a biometric check.
