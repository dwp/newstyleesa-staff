Work coach view - version 1

What we want to learn

  • How do they know about appointments? Do they use Q Flow?
  • Under what circumstances would they contact a claimant?
  • What do they use LMS for after the interview has ended?
  • Is the file uploader intuitive?
  • Do work coaches need access to the information users have submitted in the digital claim form?
  • Do they understand which status to select and when?
  • Do they know what to do after verifying a claim? If so, what do they do?
  • Do they understand that they don’t need to upload the claimant commitment and identity documents?
  • Do they enter information such as their staff number when providing a reason for disallowing a claim?
  • Do they need to be able to disallow a claim or is it enough to either leave the status or mark it as ‘new appointment needed’
  • Can they add a clerical claim?
  • When adding a clerical form, do they upload the claim form and supporting evidence separately?
  • Do they understand what ‘view or update the claim’ means?
  • Is the information in the blue header useful?
  • They spot the ‘special rules’ message
  • Do the email templates work as expected on the machines work coaches use?
  • Are the email templates intuitive?
  • Do they realise they can search by name and National Insurance number?
  • Do they choose to search by name or National Insurance number?

Scenarios to test

  1. Barney Rubble, QQ111111Z

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes
    • pension statement
    Changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence.

  2. Clark Kent, QQ112233A (special rules)

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes
    Changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence.

  3. Buzz Lightyear, QQ000011A (special rules)

    Fails to attend.

    Changes status to ‘New appointment needed’ which begins 1 month countdown to deletion of digital claim form.

  4. Lois Lane, QQ111122B

    Fails to attend.

    Changes status to ‘New appointment needed’ which begins 1 month countdown to deletion of digital claim form.

  5. Micky Mouse, QQ112233Z (part 1)

    Attends and provides:

    • fit notes
    • pension statement

    Doesn’t provide ID or SSP1.

    Changes status to ‘New appointment needed’ which begins 1 month countdown to deletion of digital claim form.

  6. Micky Mouse, QQ112233Z (part 2)

    Returns to the Jobcentre and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    Once ID check is complete, agent changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence

  7. Marge Simpson, QQ231234Z (part 1)

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes
    • pension statement

    Chooses not to sign claimant commitment.

    Changes status to ‘New appointment needed’ which begins 1 month countdown to deletion of digital claim form.

  8. Marge Simpson, QQ231234Z (part 2)

    Attends and signs claimant commitment.

    Changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence.

  9. Lex Luther, QQ001122A (part 1)

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes

    Pension statement is missing.

    Agent changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence

  10. Lex Luther, QQ001122A (part 2)

    Returns to jobcentre and provides the following:

    • fit notes
    • pension statement
    Agent uploads further supporting evidence

  11. Minnie Mouse, QQ010101A

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes

    She wasn’t sure it worked out the first time she filled in the form online, so filled it in again, but knows the information was the same on both.

    During discussions, she realises she is able to claim from an earlier date.

    Checks with claimant which form to use, edits the claim date, then changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence and changes status to ‘withdrawn’ for all other application forms.

  12. Fred Flintstone, QQ112233B (part 1)

    Attends and provides:

    • only one form of ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes

    Fit notes are missing.

    Agent completes a biometric test and changes status to ‘new appointment needed’

  13. Fred Flintstone, QQ112233B (part 2)

    Returns to jobcentre and provides the following:

    • full ID
    Agent changes status to 'verified' and uploads supporting evidence

  14. Homer Simpson, QQ112233C

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes
    • pension statements

    He’s submitted 2 claims as he needed to change some information.

    Checks with customer which form to use, then changes status to ‘verified’ and uploads supporting evidence and changes status to ‘withdrawn’ for all other application forms.

  15. Snow White, QQ121212A

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes

    She was going to fill in the form online but got a bit confused by the questions and so they stopped completing it. She decides to complete a paper form instead.

    Asks her if she’s submitted a digital application and then checks to see if she’d prefer to complete a digital or clerical form.

    Once action has been taken to complete clerical form, agent adds new clerical claim, uploads claim form and supporting evidence.

  16. Bugs Bunny, QQ123456Z

    Attends and provides:

    • ID
    • SSP1
    • fit notes

    They think they’ve completed the form online. They end up deciding to complete a paper claim form instead.

    Searches for claimant by name vs. NINO. Or agent uses Searchlight/CIS to confirm details.

    Asks customer if they have submitted a digital application and asks them to complete a digital or clerical form.

    Once action has been taken to complete clerical form, agent adds new clerical claim, uploads claim form and supporting evidence.
