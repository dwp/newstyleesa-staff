Documents to check at the appointment

Identity documents

You will need to see the following identity documents:

  • 1 primary identity document, such as a current passport, driving licence or bank card
  • 2 secondary identity documents that prove their name and address, such as a utility bill, bank statement, hospital letter or birth certificate

If they do not have their identity documents, you should do a biometric check.
