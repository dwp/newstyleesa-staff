Help with identity documents

Primary identity documents

Examples include:

  • current passport
  • driving licence
  • biometric residence permit
  • certificate of naturalisation as a British citizen
  • permanent residence card

Secondary identity documents

Examples of proof of date of birth include:

  • birth certificate
  • marriage certificate
  • NHS medical card

Examples of proof of address include a recent:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • Council Tax bill
