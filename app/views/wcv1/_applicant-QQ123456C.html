At the claimant commitment meeting, check for the following:

  • 3 identity documents
  • fit notes
  • SSP1 form (this confirms when their Statutory Sick Pay ends)
  • P45 form
  • 5 most recent weekly pay slips from Co-op
  • 2 most recent monthly pay slips from Boots
  • proof of pensions
  • proof of any permanent health insurance
