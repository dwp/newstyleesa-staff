What we learned

  • Further evidence for having full claim form details as part of the same page vs. separate browser tab
  • The tracker team assumed that marking the application as ‘verified’ would be something that the work coaches would do
  • Using the word ‘application’ instead of ‘claim‘ didin’t seem to make a difference (both were understood)
  • Participants told us that having the full application on the page instead of opening up a PDF would be more useful
  • The layout of the PDF was well received
  • The ‘Special rules’ banner was immediately noticed
