Version 2 – tested on 9 April in Belfast

Changes in this version

  • Reduced number of statuses – we now just have ‘verified’ and ‘failed to attend’ (the ‘processed’ status isn’t needed as the tracker contains this information)
  • Learned from the ‘new style’ Jobseeker’s Allowance team and followed their styling conventions so the system is more familiar to work coaches
  • Designed screens for when a user gets multiple results or no results
  • Replaced the status change buttons (and their 'undo' actions) with a separate radio list of statuses and what they mean

What we learned

  • Spaces in the National Insurance number are unhelpful
  • The ‘Upload their application to DRS’ screen was not clear – agents assumed this was being done for them by clicking the green button
  • PDF opening in the same browser tab caused issues
  • Format of PDF worked well and is easier to read than handwritten form
  • ‘Special rules’ indicator wasn’t always noticed
  • A way of identifying applications is needed for when multiple applications are submitted by the same user on the same date
